### The quirky hobby of a child
McDonald's + trains when I was a child



An FS regional train at Udine, 1997, only few years before my regular visits to the station.  Source: Marco Cacozza - Italian Wikipedia, CC BY-SA 3.0, https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=3554528

I am not able to recall whether I was already a philosopher, or if I was just spacing out by staring dully at trains.
Let's examine the first hypothesis. 

### Zeitgeist
A few years ago,  a quote in a bookmark struck me:
“The stations are an old passion of mine. I could spend whole days there, sitting in a corner, watching what happens. What other place, better than a station, reflects the spirit of a country, the state of mind of the people, its problems?”  
(Tiziano Terzani)

What are their concerns and troubles? 

### Trains as life 

People moving around, 
Think about the stories and experiences of the passengers. The young emigrate longing for home. The lover . Someone going to visit his or her beloved. 

Trains are in constant motion and change, and so is life.
